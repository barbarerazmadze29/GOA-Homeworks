Goal-Oriented Academy is a Georgian-based educational institution that offers a wide range of courses focused primarily on programming, IT skills, and personal development. The academy operates with the goal of providing students with both technical and soft skills needed in today's competitive job market.

History
Goal-Oriented Academy was founded with the aim of helping individuals become more proficient in information technology (IT) and programming. The academy offers diverse course selections, ranging from basic web development and coding to advanced fields such as data analysis, UX/UI design, and digital marketing.

Programs and Courses
GOA offers a wide array of programs tailored to students of varying skill levels, including beginners and experienced professionals. Key areas of focus include:

Web Programming:
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Angular. UX/UI Design: Training in user experience and user interface design principles. Data Analysis and SQL: Courses designed to teach data management and querying. QA Testing: Quality Assurance testing for software applications. Digital Marketing: Covering aspects such as SEO, social media marketing, and online advertising. Teaching Methodology
The academy focuses on a project-based, hands-on approach to learning. Courses are designed to prepare students for real-world applications by integrating practical assignments with theoretical concepts.
